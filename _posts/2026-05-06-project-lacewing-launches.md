Project Lacewing is now officially underway.

Lacewing is a European, open, community-driven initiative to research how AI systems handle vulnerability information — and what that means for the security of software and infrastructure that organisations across Europe depend on.

The project is led by [DIVD](https://www.divd.nl) and supported by [Schuberg Philis](https://www.schubergphilis.com) as our first sponsor. We are actively looking for additional partners — organisations that want early visibility into findings, recognition in the European security community, and the knowledge that they are on the right side of history.

## What comes next

Our first sub-project, **Project Initiation**, kicks off on 7 May 2026. From there we will move into structured research on both open-source and closed-source AI models and the software ecosystems around them.

We will publish our findings, methodology, and tooling here as work progresses.
